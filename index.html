About Me

Hey, my name is Lisa aka Owl of None and I am a 29 year old Content Creator located in Germany. As I was just working on my Phd title in Neurobiology, I decided it is time for a change. Somehow the basic idea of the internet inspires me: Sharing ideas and information with people from all around the world.

That plus my endless curiosity made me want to explore this Freelance-Content-Creator thing! If you want to accompany me with that, you will find my social media sites in the box on top of the page.

In the beginning I will experiment with different kinds of content on various social media platforms. My content represents all of my hobbies: ranging from world building, character design, societal issues like "How to live eco-friendly?" to some nerdy stuff like video games or programming.
